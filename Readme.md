# CivicMind AI

**Every Citizen a Sensor. Every Report an Action.**

CivicMind AI is an AI-powered civic issue reporting platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities. The platform enables users to report civic problems such as garbage overflow, potholes, water leakage, streetlight failures, and road damage through a simple digital workflow.

The prototype demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can help classify issues, determine priority levels, generate formal complaints, and provide authorities with actionable insights through a centralized dashboard.

---

## Problem Statement

Millions of civic issues remain unresolved due to:

* Unstructured complaint submissions
* Lack of proper issue categorization
* Delayed communication with authorities
* Poor visibility into complaint status
* Inefficient reporting processes

Citizens often do not know where or how to report issues, resulting in delays and reduced civic engagement.

---

## Solution

CivicMind AI streamlines the complaint reporting process through an intelligent workflow:

1. Citizen reports an issue.
2. AI analyzes and categorizes the problem.
3. Priority level is determined automatically.
4. A structured complaint is generated.
5. Authorities can monitor reports through a dashboard.

This creates a faster, more organized, and scalable civic issue management system.
